Frederik Hartmann

Welcome to my website!

I am a computational linguist at the University of North Texas.

My linguistic research interests concern computational modelling of linguistic change, Germanic/Indo-European historical linguistics, and language family diversification. I mainly work in developing causal models (Bayesian inference, neural networks, and agent-based models) for language evolution as a complex system.

Fields of study

  • Deep learning
  • Agent-based modelling
  • Cladistics/Phylogenetics
  • Bayesian inference
  • Diachronic linguistics
  • Linguistic complexity

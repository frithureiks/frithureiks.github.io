Frederik Hartmann

Welcome to my website!

I am a computational linguist at the University of North Texas.

I specialize in causal machine learning and causal models of language. I work in developing causal models (Bayesian inference, neural networks, and agent-based models) for language as a complex system. My linguistic research interests concern computational modelling of linguistic change, cognitive properties of language evolution, and language family diversification. .

Fields of study

  • Causal AI
  • Deep learning
  • Agent-based modelling
  • Cladistics/Phylogenetics
  • Bayesian inference
  • Diachronic linguistics
  • Linguistic complexity
